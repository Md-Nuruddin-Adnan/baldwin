Baldwin City is a city in Douglas County, Kansas, United States about 12 miles south of Lawrence and 15 miles west of Gardner. As of the 2010 census, the city population was 4,515. The city is home to Baker University, the state's oldest four-year university. Visit Baldwin City and discover what we have to offer. Historic trains, great people and a friendly downtown make Baldwin City a one of a kind place.
